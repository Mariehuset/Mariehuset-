The story

Mariehuset is an 1898 island farmhouse in the Helligvær archipelago, reachable by express ferry from Bodø. It’s a time-travel mix: modern kitchen/bath + original charm, with the quiet “island time” you only get far from the mainland.

Start your day with coffee, walk to a high point on the island, and take in a wide 360° view toward Lofoten. In summer, explore islets by kayak; in winter, watch for Northern Lights in the dark Arctic sky.

FAQ

How do we get there?

Use the express ferry from Bodø sentrum area to Helligvær. Always plan with weather and schedules.

Is this a modern luxury cabin?

It’s a historic 1898 house with modern kitchen/bath — authentic charm first, boutique comfort second.

Are towels and bed linen included?

Full sets are available, but optional—bring your own if you prefer a lower price.

Is cleaning included?

Cleaning is not included. Cleaning products are provided for guests.

Pets / service animals?

No pets or service animals are allowed.
